About the business

Wings Arms Dealers: A Legacy of Trust Wings Arms Dealer was founded in 1985 by former military officer Ananias Lekhooa, a man deeply committed to the values of safety, precision, and reliability. After serving two decades in the armed forces, Harrison saw a need for high-quality, dependable firearms that civilians could trust for personal protection and recreational use. Starting in a small workshop in Ladybrand, Free State,Wings Arms Dealers began by crafting bespoke firearms for local law enforcement and hunting enthusiasts. The company quickly earned a reputation for its meticulous craftsmanship and unwavering commitment to customer safety. Over the years, Wings Arms expanded its product line, incorporating advanced technology and materials to produce firearms that stand the test of time and use. By 2000, Wings Arms had grown from a local business into a respected name nationwide, renowned for its innovative designs and robust safety features. Today, Wings Arms remains dedicated to its founding principles, continually innovating to meet the evolving needs of its customers while upholding the highest standards of safety and reliability. Whether for personal protection, sport shooting, or collection, Wings Arms’ products are trusted by thousands across the country. Wings Arms isn’t just about selling firearms—it’s about providing peace of mind, ensuring that every product reflects the legacy of trust and excellence that Ananias Lekhooa envisioned.
